Development

1. Turnaround

Development

My character turnaround design draft taking inspiration from aspects of generic horror characters such as long sharp pointy teeth, creepy possessed eyes, a disproportionate long thin body and a stitched mouth which creates a spooky vibe to my character.

Feedback

Do you think the layout is easy to follow/ well organised?

The features looks good to the question. The layout looks easy to follow.

What don't you like about the layout?

I can't see the lines very well.

1. Turnaround improvements

Outlines

I outlined everything in my turnaround with darker lines based on previous feedback and my own experience of the lines being hard to see.

2. Torso

Torso X view (Front)

I used a cube with loop cuts to create several vertices that i dragged to shape the form of my character's torso, using my turnaround as a reference.

Torso Y view (Side)

I then switched to y view, the side profile of my character and dragged both sides of the torsos vertices in place according to my turnaround reference to give it a curved bent shape.

3. Arms

Development

To create the arms i scaled down a cube, narrowed its top vertices and brought the bottom vertices closer to the top creating an isoceles trapezoid shape.
Then i extruded the bottom vertices and narrowed down the new bottom vertices creating an arm part with a coffin like shape.
I copy pasted several of these arm parts and joined them together to create the arms bicep and forearm and then used the extrude and scale/move tool to shape the hand.

4. Legs

Development

Creating the legs for my low poly character was pretty straightforward.
I used multiple of the previously made arm parts joined together to create my legs, i then shaped them to resemble the legs of my character using loop cuts and vertices.
finally i extruded the bottom of the leg and used vertex select to shape it into a foot

5. Head

Development

To create the head, i used loop cuts on a scaled down cube to shape a head.
I also made a neck using the same method.

6. Armature

Development

Finally after creating my character's model i rigged the armature
however there was an error during testing
when i moved the spine my legs and arms change positions but not my head which wasn't the case.
